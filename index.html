The Goal at a Glance

Four Focus Areas of SDG 7

SDG 7 rests on four pillars that work together to deliver clean, affordable, modern energy for everyone.

power

Energy Access

Reliable, affordable electricity for every community — including rural and underserved areas.

solar_power

Renewable Energy

A bigger share of solar, wind, hydro and other clean sources in the world's energy mix.

eco

Energy Efficiency

Doing the same work with less energy — cutting waste, costs and emissions all at once.

science

Clean Technology

Modern, low-emission tech that supports a healthier planet for the next generations.
