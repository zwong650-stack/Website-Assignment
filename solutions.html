Get Started

Five Steps Anyone Can Take

A simple roadmap from awareness to action. Pick the steps that match your situation.

  1. Step 01 · Learn

    Understand SDG 7 and your own usage

    Spend 15 minutes reading our About SDG 7 page and check your last electricity bill. Knowing where you start makes every other step measurable.

  2. Step 02 · Save

    Cut waste at home this week

    Switch off standby devices, use daylight, set the AC to 24°C, and replace one bulb with an LED. These changes alone often cut bills by 10–15%.

  3. Step 03 · Switch

    Choose efficient appliances

    When something needs replacing, look for a high energy-rating label. The price difference pays itself back in less than two years for most large appliances.

  4. Step 04 · Solar

    Explore solar for your home or community

    Get a quick quote from a local installer. Even renters can support the shift by joining community-solar programmes or asking landlords about rooftop access.

  5. Step 05 · Share

    Spread awareness

    Share this site, talk to friends and family, run an awareness booth at school, or contact us to join the Solaris student campaign.

Daily Habits

Energy-Saving Tips That Add Up

Eight small habits anyone can apply at home, school, or work. Each one cuts waste and supports SDG 7.

01

Switch off when not in use

Turn off lights, fans, and electronics when leaving a room to avoid wasting electricity.

02

Use natural daylight

Open curtains and use sunlight during the day instead of switching on indoor lights.

03

Unplug after use

Unplug chargers and small appliances when not needed to stop "phantom" energy use.

04

Choose efficient devices

Pick energy-efficient bulbs and appliances — they use less power and last longer.

05

Use AC wisely

Set air-conditioning to a moderate 24°C and clean filters regularly for efficient cooling.

06

Wash full loads

Run washing machines and dishwashers only on full loads to maximise water and energy use.

07

Cool food before fridging

Let hot food reach room temperature before storing — the fridge then uses less energy.

08

Support renewable energy

Choose, share, or support solar, wind and other clean energy solutions where available.
